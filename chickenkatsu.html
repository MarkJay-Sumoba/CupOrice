Chicken Katsu

Chicken Katsu is our Japanese signature bowl. Chicken breast fillet breaded with flour, egg, and Japanese panko breadcrumbs, then deep-fried until golden brown, served with your choice of rice and tonkatsu sauce.The crispy crust and the flavorful juicy meat bring great satisfaction to every bite.
These tender and crispy panko-breaded cutlets are surprisingly delicious.


Chicken Katsu Main Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • French Onion
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Tonkatsu Sauce
  • Green Onion
  • Toasted Sesame
