Vege-O-Paella

This dish is a classic Spanish meal featuring saffron rice, tomatoes, onions, green peas & chickpeas, and peppers. Garished with parsley and lemon, the Vege-O-Paella has bold flavour without being too spicy.

Ingredients

  • Red Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Chickpeas
  • Green Peas

Toppings

  • Parsley
  • Lemon Juice
  • Saffron
