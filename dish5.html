Shrimp-O-Risotto

Delectable shrimp risotto that is sure to satisfy any seafood lover's cravings. This creamy and rich risotto is infused with the flavors of white wine, parmesan cheese, and aromatic garlic. It is then topped off with succulent shrimp that have been perfectly cooked to tender perfection.

  • Lemon Juice
