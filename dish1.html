Curry-O-Rice

Indulge in the timeless charm of the beloved classic: Japanese-style curry rice. Delight in its sweetness, velvety texture, and a harmonious blend of curry flavours meticulously crafted with organic potatoes, carrots, and onions. Served alongside with fragrant Japanese white rice, Curry-O-Rice is a vegetarian delight that allows you the freedom to enhance it with your preferred protein. Embrace the authentic simplicity of this dish as it captures the essence of Japanese culture.

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onions

Toppings

  • Choice of your meat
