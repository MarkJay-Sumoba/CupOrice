Tonkatsu-O-Rice

Tonkatsu is a fried Japanese pork cutlet that's usually served with a sweet and savory spiced fruit sauce. Cup-O-Rice recipe features a tender thick-cut rib-chop that's been dredged in panko breadcrumbs and fried until shatteringly crisp on the outside, and juicy on the inside. Served in a bowl of cooked rice with cheese or egg topping as an option.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Panko (Japanese breadcrumbs)
  • Egg
  • Flour
  • Salt & Pepper

Toppings

  • Fried Egg
  • Cheese
  • Green oinons
  • Sesame seeds
